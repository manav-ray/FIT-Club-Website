About us

Mission Statement

The goal of FIT club is to build a community of students who are committed to improving both their physical and mental health. FIT club will host guest speakers who are experts in fitness or mental health (once a month ideally), organize weekly workouts, and participate in and/or raise money for community events and philanthropic causes.
